## How to sign

You sign this CLA **once**, on your first pull request to this repository.
When you open a PR, the CLA Assistant bot will comment with instructions. To
sign, post the following as a comment on your pull request, exactly:

```
I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
```

Your signature is recorded in `signatures/version1/cla.json` on the
`cla-signatures` branch of this repository. You only need to sign once;
subsequent pull requests are recognized automatically.
